🕌 About Islamic Chat

Founder & Owner: Sehan Shahid

Islamic Chat was born from a simple idea: make authentic Islamic knowledge accessible to everyone, using the power of AI. As a solo developer and a Muslim, I wanted to create a tool that answers questions about Quran, Hadith, daily life, and Islamic history – without bias, without ads, and with respect for traditional scholarship.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, educational AI companion for Muslims and non‑Muslims alike. We aim to bridge the gap between modern technology and timeless Islamic guidance.

How It Works

Our AI is trained on authentic sources: Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Quranic tafsir, and classical fiqh books. It gives answers based on evidence, not opinion. For personal religious rulings (fatwas), we always advise consulting a qualified scholar.

Why Free + Premium?

Everyone gets 10 AI conversations per day – completely free. Premium ($10/month) unlocks unlimited chat, deep tafsir, audio Quran, and a personalized learning plan. This helps sustain the project.

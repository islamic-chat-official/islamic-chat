💰 Refund Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Premium Subscription

Islamic Chat Premium costs $10/month and auto‑renews. You can cancel anytime from your account settings or by emailing support@islamicchat.online. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Refunds

  • First‑time subscribers: You may request a full refund within 7 days of your first payment if you have not used more than 10 premium chats. Contact support with your email and reason.
  • Subsequent months: No refunds for partial months. If you cancel, you will retain access until the end of the paid period.
  • Technical issues: If the service is unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours, you may request a pro‑rated refund.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@islamicchat.online with the subject "Refund Request". Include your account email and the date of payment. We will respond within 3 business days.

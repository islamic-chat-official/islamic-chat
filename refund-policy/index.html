💰 Refund Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This refund policy applies to all paid subscriptions of Islamic Chat Premium.

Premium Subscription

Islamic Chat Premium costs $10/month and auto‑renews every 30 days. You can cancel anytime from your account settings or by emailing support@islamicchat.online. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle – you will not be charged again.

Refund Eligibility

  • First‑time subscribers: You may request a full refund within 7 days of your first payment, provided you have used fewer than 10 premium AI conversations. Contact support with your email and reason.
  • Subsequent months: No refunds for partial months. If you cancel after a renewal, you will retain access until the end of that paid period.
  • Technical issues: If the service is completely unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours, you may request a pro‑rated refund for the downtime.
  • Duplicate payments: If you are accidentally charged twice, we will refund the extra charge immediately upon verification.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@islamicchat.online with the subject line "Refund Request". Include:

  • The email address used for your Islamic Chat account
  • The date of the payment
  • The reason for the refund request (optional but helpful)

You will receive a response within 3 business days. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5‑10 business days.

Cancellation Instructions

You can cancel your premium subscription at any time:

  • Inside the app: Go to Settings → Manage Subscription → Cancel
  • Or email support@islamicchat.online with "Cancel Subscription" in the subject line

You will receive a confirmation email once cancelled. No further charges will occur.

Contact Us

For any refund or billing questions, please reach out to support@islamicchat.online. We are committed to fair and transparent handling of all requests.
